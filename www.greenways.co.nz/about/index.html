Always Greenways

Greenways is family owned and operated, we have proudly been growing grapes since 1956. With a bit of inspiration, Greenways has come from the humble origins in Te Kauwhata to now being a product stocked in good stores nationwide. Our signature product is no doubt our 100% grape juice, which really has to be tasted to be believed.

Over time we have also added more varieties to the mix, all our juices have no preservatives, no colourings and no added sugar. That's the way we like it. With all our products, we keep it simple and let nature and the countryside of New Zealand do what it does best, produce great fruit for making outstanding juices.

We are proud of our juices and guarantee their quality. At Greenways, we are passionate about juice and providing them to our consumers in the most natural state possible, without all the nasties. We strive to keep making the best juices for many years to come.

Always Greenways. Always Quality.
